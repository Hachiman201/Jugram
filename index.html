About the program

The Nithub Accelerator is built for digital startups across Africa looking to move from early traction to scale. Over four months, selected founders will gain access to tailored growth resources, mentorship, and strategic ecosystem support — including technical advisory and tools worth thousands of dollars

What You’ll Get

Comprehensive Support for Your Startup Journey


Expert-led masterclasses

Connect with experienced entrepreneurs and industry leaders who will provide personalized guidance and support.


Dedicated mentors for guidance

Gain access to funding opportunities, workspaces, and other essential resources to fuel your growth.


Co-working & meeting room access

Join a vibrant community of fellow founders, investors, and mentors to expand your network and collaborate.


Demo Day with investors and partners

Showcase your venture to a curated audience of top-tier investors, corporate partners, and ecosystem leaders at Demo Day.


Post-program support for scale

Gain continued access to growth resources, strategic guidance, and growth tools to support your startup’s scale even after the program ends.


Tools & resources

Accelerate your growth with a comprehensive suite of tools tailored to meet your startup needs.

Who Should Apply

  • Early-stage startups (with MVP and initial traction)
  • Digital solutions only (web, mobile, SaaS, etc.)
  • At least one technical co-founder
  • Operating in or targeting the African market
